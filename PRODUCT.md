# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primary user is D as the first MASTER with sovereign control over catalog, access, release timing, and sharing terms. Secondary users are L and invited collaborators who operate within explicit permission boundaries. The core job is to manage and present music as artist-owned infrastructure rather than platform-owned distribution.

## Product Purpose

Pleasant Soul Collective exists to give independent artists full control over how their work is stored, shared, and experienced. Success means creators can manage permissions, release intent, and audience access without algorithmic pressure or interventionist platform behavior.

## Brand Personality

Precision, synthesis, discernment. The tone is cinematic and intentional, with authority grounded in creator sovereignty.

## Anti-references

Performative interfaces that optimize for spectacle over control. Interventionist product behavior that steers artist decisions through algorithms, engagement loops, or manipulative defaults.

## Design Principles

1. Artist sovereignty is the product contract, not a settings toggle.
2. Control surfaces must be clear, deterministic, and reversible when possible.
3. Visual direction should reinforce authorship, not distract from the work.
4. Every workflow must preserve creator intent from upload to access.
5. System language should signal trust, precision, and restraint.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA baseline, keyboard-first operation for core flows, and reduced-motion support for users with vestibular sensitivity.
